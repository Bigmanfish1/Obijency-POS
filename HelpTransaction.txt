-The "barcode" button will allow you to go to the transaction page where you will be able to scan products using a barcode scanner 
to read the various details of products in the business
-The "additional" button will take you the additional transaction page where you will be able to input a transaction that ins't a manual
or a barcode transaction such as a debtor paying of their debt
-The "manual" button will take you to the manul transaction page where you will be able to manually input every detail of the product
based on the required fields
-The "main menu" button will take you to the main menu screen of the program where you will be able to navigate to any screen in the program
-The "help" button will take you to the help page of the screen you are currently on
